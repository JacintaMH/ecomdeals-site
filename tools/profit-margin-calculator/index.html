What's a healthy margin?
Under 10% — Thin. Any spike in ad costs or returns could push you into the red. Revisit your pricing or COGS.
10–20% — Workable but tight. Most stores aim to improve this over time through better supplier pricing and customer LTV.
20–40% — Healthy. You have room to invest in growth, absorb returns, and weather seasonal dips.
40%+ — Strong. Typical of digital products, high-margin consumables, or premium DTC brands.
