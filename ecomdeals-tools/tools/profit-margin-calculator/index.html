What's a healthy margin?
→
Under 10% — Thin. Any increase in ad costs or returns could push you into the red. Revisit your pricing or COGS.
→
10–20% — Workable but tight. Most ecommerce businesses aim to improve this over time through better supplier pricing and LTV.
→
20–40% — Healthy. You have room to invest in growth, absorb returns, and weather seasonal dips.
→
40%+ — Strong. Typical of digital products, high-margin consumables, or DTC brands with premium positioning.
